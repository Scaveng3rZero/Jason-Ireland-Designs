Portfolio

Ten refined works that represent my range and development as a designer.

This portfolio presents a curated selection of graphic design work that reflects my abilities across branding, layout, digital design, image-making, photography, and visual storytelling. Each project has been selected to show both variety and cohesion while demonstrating growth in technical execution, design thinking, and presentation.

Selected Works

A portfolio built for clarity, variety, and presentation

The projects below are arranged to balance structured design work with more expressive creative pieces. Together, they reflect a range of applications while maintaining a unified visual identity and professional standard of presentation.

Advertising Design

Amethyst Bay Resort & Spa Ad

A full-page luxury travel advertisement designed to promote a fictional resort brand. This piece emphasizes hierarchy, atmosphere, image selection, and refined layout to create a polished editorial-style advertisement with strong visual appeal.

UI / Digital Design

Fitness Tracker App

A digital interface concept created to organize workout tracking, planning, and progress logging. This project demonstrates usability, consistency, layout control, and clear visual hierarchy in a screen-based format.

Promotional Design

Play Has No Limits

A gaming-themed promotional composition focused on mood, motion, and dramatic visual impact. The design uses lighting, contrast, and layered imagery to create energy while reinforcing a strong entertainment aesthetic.

Typography

Typographic Portrait

A portrait created entirely through text arrangement, scale variation, and repetition. This project explores how typography can function as both language and image through contrast, spacing, and composition.

Brand Identity

Vulture Entertainment Logo

A logo design created for a game publishing concept, refined for stronger presentation and print readiness. The mark focuses on silhouette, contrast, memorability, and brand tone through a bold monochromatic treatment.

Identity Design

O-Fam Logo

A family-oriented identity concept developed around symbolic form and simplified branding. This piece reflects logo construction, shape relationships, and the challenge of refining concept into a cleaner visual mark.

Photo Manipulation

PlayStation 5 Concept Ad

A photo-based advertising concept that transforms a gaming scene into a more immersive promotional image. The design combines photographic editing, compositing, and visual effects to create depth, motion, and branded energy.

Photography

Black and White Portrait

A portrait study focused on mood, lighting, and tonal range through monochrome photography. This piece demonstrates control of value, composition, and emotional presence using simplified visual elements.

Product Photography

Gaming Peripheral Product Shot

A product-focused photographic composition designed to highlight form, texture, and lighting. This image emphasizes presentation, controlled framing, and visual contrast to give the object a more commercial feel.

Illustration

Illustration Study

An expressive drawing-based work included to represent traditional visual foundations within the portfolio. This piece highlights line control, stylization, contrast, and character-driven visual storytelling.

Portfolio Structure

Why these projects are presented in this order

The sequence begins with polished design pieces that establish professionalism and visual control, then expands into typography, branding, image manipulation, photography, and illustration to show range without losing cohesion. This order helps create a narrative of both technical skill and creative development.
